My Favorite TV Shows

Top 5 Shows of all Time

1. Mayor of Kingstown

An ex-con turned police informant
trys keeping the peace in the small town
of kingston, MI.

2. Band of Brothers

A group of men in WWI find themselves
pinned down in enemy territory and have to
stick together to survive.

3. The Office

Mocumentary of a small town paper company
following the lives of the employees and the
rise and fall of the paper industry.

4. Dexter

A serial killer turned forensic scientist
helps feed his desire of killing by hunting
criminals and delivering justice his way.

Friends

following the lives of 6 friends
living in new york, falling in love
and surviving in the big city.
